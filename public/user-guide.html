Overview

The Mobility Dashboard is a browser-based operations tool designed to support transportation monitoring during World Cup 2026 matches at NRG Stadium. It provides a unified view of METRO bus and rail service, live traffic conditions, weather alerts, and traffic camera feeds — all updated in real time.

🚌
METRO Transit
Live bus and scheduled rail departures for routes serving NRG Stadium
🚦
INRIX Traffic
Real-time incident alerts and speed segments on key corridors
📷
Camera Feeds
Live TxDOT and TranStar camera streams sorted by proximity to NRG
⛅
Weather
NWS observations and active alert badges with severity color-coding

Tips & Best Practices

  • 🏟️
    Game Day Preparation Open the METRO Live panel 90 minutes before kickoff to see scheduled rail departures. Rail entries always show a SCHED badge — this is expected since METRO does not publish real-time GPS for rail.
  • 🚦
    Traffic Monitoring Watch for red segments on I-610 South Loop or US-59 near NRG. Use the INRIX Traffic panel on the right for a list of active incidents. Click any incident to zoom the map to its exact location.
  • 📷
    Camera Feeds Feeds are sorted closest to NRG Stadium first — the top entries cover the immediate stadium area. Clicking a camera shows its pin on the map. Click it again to dismiss.
  • ⚠️
    Weather Alerts Hover over colored alert badges in the header to read the full NWS headline. Red = Extreme, Orange = Severe, Yellow = Moderate, Blue = Minor.
  • 🗺️
    Map Navigation Use the Home button (top-right) to quickly return to the default Houston view after zooming in on an incident, camera, or transit vehicle. Switch basemaps in the Map Details panel for different viewing contexts.
  • 🖥️
    Best Viewing Use full-screen or a maximized browser window. The dashboard is designed for a 1920×1080 or larger display for optimal readability of all panels simultaneously.
