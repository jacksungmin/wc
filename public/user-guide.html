Overview

The Mobility Dashboard is a browser-based operations tool designed to support transportation monitoring during World Cup 2026 matches at NRG Stadium. It provides a unified view of METRO bus and rail service, live traffic conditions, weather alerts, and traffic camera feeds — all updated in real time.

🚌
METRO Transit
Live bus and scheduled rail departures organized by route category
🚦
INRIX Traffic
Real-time incident alerts and speed conditions on key corridors
📷
Camera Feeds
Live TxDOT and TranStar camera feeds sorted by proximity to NRG
⛅
Weather
NWS conditions and active alert badges with severity color-coding

Tips & Best Practices

  • 🏟️
    Game Day Preparation Expand the Bus to NRG Stadium group 90 minutes before kickoff to monitor inbound routes. Rail entries always show a "scheduled" label — this is expected since METRO does not publish live vehicle tracking for rail.
  • 👁️
    Declutter the Map Use the eye icon on each route group to show or hide those pins on the map. Focus on just the corridors relevant to your current situation without turning off everything else.
  • ✈️
    Airport & Fan Festival Routes Route 40 appears under both Fan Festival and Airport groups since it serves both corridors. Routes 500 (Downtown Direct) and 102 (Bush IAH) connect airports to downtown on match days.
  • 🚦
    Traffic Monitoring Watch for red road lines on I-610 South Loop or US-59 near NRG. Use the INRIX Traffic panel on the right for a list of active incidents. Click any incident to zoom the map to its exact location.
  • 📷
    Camera Feeds Turn on the Cameras layer in Map Details to see all camera pins, or click a camera in the right panel to jump directly to it. Feeds are sorted closest to NRG Stadium first.
  • ⚠️
    Weather Alerts Hover over colored alert badges in the header to read the full NWS headline. Red = Extreme, Orange = Severe, Yellow = Moderate, Blue = Minor.
  • 🖥️
    Best Viewing Use full-screen or a maximized browser window. The dashboard is designed for a 1920×1080 or larger display for optimal readability of all panels simultaneously.
